What is a Fork Bomb?

A fork bomb is like a digital rabbit that keeps multiplying until your computer runs out of resources!

Step 1: Creation

A fork bomb starts as a simple program that creates a copy of itself.

Step 2: Multiplication

Each copy creates more copies, growing exponentially.

Step 3: System Impact

Your computer slows down as it runs out of memory and processing power.

Visual Example

Process
Copy 1
Copy 2

And this goes on forever until there is some memory left.....
